Payments Use Cases
Copy

Wise supports a range of use cases for it's Send Money product depending on who you're sending money for.

Explore the guides below to find the right approach for your business: sending your own funds as an enterprise, facilitating cross-border payments as a licensed bank or financial institution, or embedded payments into your solution to enable your customers to make payments.

Enterprise Payouts

How to make business payments via API from a Wise account.

Correspondent Payouts

How licensed banks and financial institutions can send money on behalf of their customers via API.

Embedded Payouts

How businesses without financial licenses can send money on behalf of their customers via API.

Affiliate Partners

How to display prices and refer customers to Wise to earn money via our affiliate partner programme.
