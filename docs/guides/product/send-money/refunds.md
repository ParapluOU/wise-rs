Refund Recipients
Copy

In the case of a bounce back, Wise will need to refund back to the sender. There are multiple methods to refund to the sender:

Refund to Bank Account

Directly refund to a sender's bank account.

Refund to Balance

Directly refund to a sender's Wise balance.

Net Settlement

Settle multiple transfers in one efficient payment.
