Send Money Flow Overview

The process for creating a transfer includes the following steps:

Quote creation

Create a quote that includes your source and target currencies and the amount you want to transfer.

Recipient Creation

Create or lookup the recipient/beneficiary that you want to send the funds to.

Transfer creation

Create the transfer, adding any additional details as required.

Fund the transfer

Use one of multiple methods to fund the transfer and begin the processing.
