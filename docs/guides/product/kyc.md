Onboarding & KYC
Copy

These guides are designed to help you or your customers onboard with a Wise account and verify identies.

Partner Accounts

For Partners that want to use their own Wise account to create transfers or access other Wise products.

Partner KYC

For licensed institutions, and those with trusted outsourced KYC partner status undertaking their own customer identity checks.

Wise KYC

For unlicensed partners that require Wise KYC tools to help them complete their customer identity checks.

We recommend contacting us before you begin your integration to ensure you select the right option.
